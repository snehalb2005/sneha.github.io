Transport Issues for Tourists

SDG Target 11

Focus:Sustainable Cities and Communities

SDG Indicator 11.2.1:Proportion of population that has convenient access to public transport, disaggregated by age, sex, and persons with disabilities. It emphasizes increasing coverage and efficiency of transport systems to promote equitable access for all users, including tourists.

Poor Accessibility for Tourists with Special Needs

SDG Target 10

Focus:Reduced Inequalities

SDG Indicator 8.5.2:Proportion of people living below 50% of median income, by age, sex, and persons with disabilities. While focused on inclusion, accessibility for tourists with special needs aligns with universal design principles, ensuring equitable access to services, including tourism infrastructure.
